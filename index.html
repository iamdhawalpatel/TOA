Chronological vs Biological

  • Your chronological age is simply how long you've been alive.
  • It's measured by the time since your birth.
  • Your biological age reflects how well your body is functioning compared to others.
  • It's based on biomarkers that reflect the health of your cells.
  • And if you've wondered, it yes, you can change it.

What Makes a Great BioAge?

A good biological age is the same as your chronological age. A great BioAge is lower than your chronological age.
